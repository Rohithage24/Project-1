Title: "The Bhagavad Gita"

Author: Unknown (Ancient Indian scripture)
Description:

A sacred Hindu scripture, "The Bhagavad Gita" is a conversation between Prince Arjuna and the god Krishna, who serves as his charioteer. It addresses the concepts of duty, righteousness, and the paths to spiritual realization.

Title: "The Ramayana"

Author: Valmiki
Description:
An ancient Indian epic, "The Ramayana" narrates the journey of Prince Rama as he rescues his wife Sita from the demon king Ravana with the help of Hanuman and an army of monkeys. The epic explores themes of dharma, devotion, and loyalty.

Title: "The Mahabharata"

Author: Vyasa
Description:
A vast epic, "The Mahabharata" contains the Bhagavad Gita and tells the story of the Kuru dynasty. It includes the great Kurukshetra War and explores complex moral dilemmas, duty, and the nature of reality.

Title: "My Gita"

Author: Devdutt Pattanaik
Description:
In "My Gita," Devdutt Pattanaik offers a personal and philosophical interpretation of the Bhagavad Gita. The book explores the timeless teachings of the Gita, providing insights into life, duty, and spirituality.
